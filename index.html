Society Goals

Presentation Expert

Presentations are the most important aspect of the IT industry that an intern should be skilled in and we look this skill as an important goal.

Skilled Resume

Making each member aware about the technology prevailing in the IT industry and helping them achieve those skills to fit in resume.

Placement Ready

The ultimate Goal is to make an individual ready for the Placement that every student thrives for.

Events

Events are the days of celebration when the members come together to show their team work and responsibility. The events that our society organizes are linked to technology and the greatest engineering expertised inventions of computer engineers.

Coding

CODE WAR

Code War is the annual coding event of the society and the winner gets the tag of coder of the year on our website.

Gaming

PubG

PubG is the annual gaming event organised by our society in which the winner can redeem the cash prize and t-shirts.

Fun-Tech Quiz

Qates

Qates is the fun-tech Quiz event of the society which is organised to check the tech awareness of the students.

Workshop

Slackware

Slackware is an exciting workshop that include making viruses, the remote sensing of different devices and other exciting hacks.
